From the above discussion, it comes out that in the advertisement\n\nmultiple &#x27;terms and conditions&#x27; were jotted down. In case of other posts\n\n&#x27;experience&#x27; was directly linked with the post but it was not so in case of\n\nITA/ITO/CDFO. The recruitment board after scrutiny of documents initially\n\naccepted experience of short-listed candidates and issued provisional select\n\nlist. The experience prescribed for the post of ITO/ITA and CDFO is same.\n\nThe respondent has accepted same experience for the posts of ITO/ITA and\n\nrejected for the post of CDFO. Post of ITO is feeder cadre of CDFO. No\n\noral/practical test was prescribed and written test comprised of different\n\nsubjects directly linked with post. There is no material disclosing that\n\ncandidature of everyone was rejected in the public interest. The\n\nrespondent after releasing provisional list of selected candidates decided to\n\nlink experience with post which culminating in rejection of all the candidates.\n\n\n</p><p data-structure=\"Conclusion\" id=\"p_81\">37. The\n\nrespondent was duty bound to complete recruitment process as per\n\nadvertisement. Thus, the respondents are hereby directed to reconsider case of\n\nall the short-listed candidates as per terms and conditions of the\n\nadvertisement.
