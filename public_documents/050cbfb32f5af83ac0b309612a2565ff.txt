No. - 4901 of 2022\n \n\n \nApplicant :- Neeraj Nishad\n \nOpposite Party :- State of U.P. and Another\n \nCounsel for Applicant :- Dhananjai Rai,Vibhu Rai\n \nCounsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.,Nilesh Kesharwani,Shyam Kishore Tripathi\n \n\n \nHon&#x27;ble Raj Beer Singh,J.\n \n</pre><p data-structure=\"PetArg\" id=\"p_1\">Heard learned counsel for the applicant, learned counsel for the complainant, learned A.G.A. for the State and perused the record.\n</p> \n<p data-structure=\"Issue\" id=\"p_2\">The present application has been moved seeking anticipatory bail in Case Crime no. 0107 of 2022, under <a href=\"/doc/92983/\" id=\"a_1\">Section 306</a> I.P.C., Police Station George Town, District Prayagraj, with the prayer that in the event of arrest, applicant may be released on bail.\n</p> \n<p data-structure=\"Facts\" id=\"p_3\">According to the prosecution version, the son of complainant has solemnized marriage with co-accused Shalini Nishad on 03.04.2022 but she was having illicit relations with co-accused Sandeep and applicant Neeraj Nishad and that earlier they have uploaded the obscene photographs of co-accused Shalini Nishad by hacking the facebook account of deceased, however, at that time a compromise has taken place on the intervention of police. It was alleged that applicant and co-accused Sandeep Nishad were having relations with wife of deceased and due to this reasons on 04.04.2022 the deceased has committed suicide.\n</p> \n<p data-structure=\"PetArg\" id=\"p_4\"><span class=\"citetext\" data-sentiment=\"PARTY\" data-docid=\"116099304\" id=\"span_1\"><span class=\"citetext\" data-sentiment=\"PARTY\" data-docid=\"70756725\" id=\"span_2\">It has been argued by the learned counsel for the applicant that applicant is innocent and he has an apprehension that he may be arrested in the above-mentioned case, whereas there is no credible evidence against him and that applicant has no criminal antecedents and that no coercive process has been issued against the applicant so far. It was stated that except bald allegation made against the applicant, there is no evidence against him. No such electronic evidence has been collected that the facebook ID of deceased was hacked by applicant. Learned counsel has referred the suicide note left by deceased and submitted that merely a bald allegation has been made that applicant and co-accused Sandeep Nishad are responsible for his suicide. Learned counsel has referred the case of Arnab Manoranjan Goswami vs. The state of Maharashtra &amp; Ors. criminal appeal no. 742 of 2020, <a href=\"/doc/70756725/\" id=\"a_2\">Velladurai vs. State</a> represented by the Inspector of Police criminal appeal no. 953 of 2021 and <a href=\"/doc/116099304/\" id=\"a_3\">Anand Singh &amp; Ors vs. State of U.P. &amp; Ors.</a> application<a href=\"/doc/1679850/\" id=\"a_4\"> u/s 482</a> Cr.P.C. no. 19450 of 2010 decided on 10.03.2021 and submitted that the ingredients of <a href=\"/doc/92983/\" id=\"a_5\">Section 306</a> IPC are not made out. The applicant undertakes to co-operate during investigation and trial and he would appear as and when required by the investigating agency or Court. It has been stated that in case, the applicant is granted anticipatory bail, he shall not misuse the liberty of bail and will co-operate during investigation and would obey all conditions of bail.</span></span>\n</p> \n<p data-structure=\"RespArg\" id=\"p_5\">Learned A.G.A. and learned counsel for the complainant have opposed the application for anticipatory bail and argued that in the first information report as well as in the suicide note, applicant has been categorically named by the deceased. Learned counsel for the complainant submitted that applicant has hacked the facebook account of deceased and posted obscene photographs of his wife. The investigation of the case is going on and there may be requirement of custodial interrogation of applicant and the question of recovery of electronic gadget, by which the facebook ID of deceased was hacked, is also involved. \n</p> \n<p data-structure=\"CDiscource\" id=\"p_6\">Considering submissions of learned counsel for the parties, nature of accusations, stage of investigation and attending facts and circumstances of the case, the applicant is not entitled for anticipatory bail. Hence the present application for anticipatory bail is hereby rejected.\n</p> \n<p data-structure=\"Conclusion\" id=\"p_7\">Order Date :- 12.7.2022\n \nA.
